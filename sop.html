SOP & Regulations

Standard Operating Procedures, Rules & Regulations for the International Group of Achievers.

Rules & Regulations

1. General Guidelines
  • All participants must provide accurate and genuine information.
  • IGA holds the right to verify and validate all submissions.
  • Any false information may lead to disqualification.
2. Registration Rules
  • Registration must be completed within the given deadline.
  • Fees (if applicable) are non-refundable.
  • Registration is considered valid only after full payment & confirmation.
3. Selection & Evaluation
  • Selection is based on merit, performance, and achievements.
  • The jury panel’s decision is final and cannot be challenged.
  • IGA ensures transparency and fairness in evaluation.
4. Code of Conduct
  • Participants must maintain professional behavior at all times.
  • No misconduct, discrimination, or disrespect will be tolerated.
  • Violation may result in removal from the program/event.
5. Event Participation
  • Participants must follow event schedules and guidelines.
  • Any delay or absence should be informed in advance.
  • IGA is not responsible for personal travel or accommodation issues unless specified.
6. Media & Promotion
  • IGA reserves the right to use photos/videos for promotional purposes.
  • Participants agree to allow social media and marketing usage.
7. Certification & Awards
  • Certificates/Awards will be issued only to eligible participants.
  • No duplication or misuse of certificates is allowed.
  • IGA has the right to revoke awards in case of misconduct.
8. Communication Policy
  • Official communication will be through WhatsApp / Email.
  • Response time: within 24–48 hours.
9. Refund & Cancellation Policy
  • Fees once paid are non-refundable and non-transferable.
  • Event changes (date/venue) may occur due to unavoidable circumstances.
10. Amendments
  • IGA reserves the right to update or modify rules at any time without prior notice.

The International Group of Achievers is dedicated to recognizing, celebrating, and empowering global excellence across all industries.
